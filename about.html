About RBSMUN

Discover the history and values behind one of the most prestigious Model United Nations conferences in Nepal.

A Legacy of Diplomacy

Rato Bangala School Model United Nations (RBSMUN) was established in 2005. At a time when the concept of Model UN was still nascent in Nepal, RBSMUN took the bold step of creating a platform for high school students to engage in international diplomacy.

Over the past two decades, we have grown from a modest gathering of local schools to an international-scale event, hosting over 500 delegates annually. Our entirely student-run conference prides itself on rigorous academic standards, immersive committee experiences, and fostering a community of future global leaders.
