Conference Details

Everything you need to know about logistics, scheduling, and registration.

📍 Venue

Rato Bangala School
Patan Dhoka, Lalitpur, Nepal

The conference will take place across our academic blocks, featuring state-of-the-art halls for the General Assembly and Plenary sessions.

👔 Dress Code

Western Business Attire (WBA)

  • Suits, ties, blazers, and formal trousers.
  • Formal skirts/dresses (knee-length or longer).
  • National attire is deeply encouraged on the final day of the conference.
